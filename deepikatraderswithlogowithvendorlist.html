About Deepika Traders

Deepika Traders, located in Vadodara, has been a prominent player in the scrap trading business for the last 30 years. We specialize in purchasing scrap from various companies and supplying to other scrap dealers in the market for recycling purposes.

We deal in all kinds of waste scrap, offering comprehensive solutions for responsible waste management and recycling.

Our organization is professionally managed and provides certified scrap disposal services to a diverse range of clients across commercial, industrial, and residential segments.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and environmentally responsible scrap trading company in Gujarat, setting industry standards for sustainable waste management while creating value for our stakeholders and contributing to a cleaner, greener planet.

Our Mission

To provide professional, reliable, and certified scrap disposal services that help businesses and communities manage waste responsibly. We are committed to promoting recycling, reducing landfill waste, and creating a circular economy through ethical trading practices and sustainable solutions.
